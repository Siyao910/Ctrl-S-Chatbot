Learning Modules

Understand waste before you sort it

Complete all three modules to earn the Knowledge Champion badge.

01

Reduce First

Choose reusables, repair items, and avoid unnecessary packaging.

  • Bring a reusable cup and bottle.
  • Borrow or repair before buying new.
02

Recycle Right

Empty, clean, and dry containers help recycling systems work.

  • Check local rules for accepted materials.
  • Keep food and liquids out of recycling.
03

Special Waste

Batteries and electronics need collection points, not regular bins.

  • Use campus e-waste collection sites.
  • Never put batteries in household recycling.
